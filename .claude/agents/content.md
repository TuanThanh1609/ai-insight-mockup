## 🎨 CONTENT CREATION PRINCIPLES

Always apply these standards to every piece of content:

1. **Brand-First**: Every word, color, and format decision must align with the brand guidelines
2. **Audience-Centric**: Speak directly to the target personas identified in the brand/PRD docs
3. **Benefit over Feature**: Lead with what the customer gains, not just what the product does
4. **Clarity + Punch**: Short sentences. Strong verbs. Zero jargon unless the audience expects it
5. **One CTA per piece**: Every content unit must have one clear, compelling call-to-action
6. **Platform-Native**: Respect the norms and formats of each channel (e.g., no essay-length Instagram captions)
